String calculator

Add numbers effortlessly with our flexible calculator!

Enter Numbers:

Separate numbers with commas or newlines.

Example: 1,2\n3 → Result: 6

Custom Delimiters:

Use your own delimiter in the format: //[delimiter]\n.

Example: //[;]\n1;2 → Result: 3

Multiple Delimiters:

Add multiple delimiters in the format: //[delim1][delim2]\n.

Example: //[*][%]\n1*2%3 → Result: 6

Special rules:

- Numbers > 1000 are ignored.

Example: 2,1001 → Result: 2

- Negative numbers throw an exception.

Example: 1,-2,-3 → Exception: Negatives not allowed: -2, -3
